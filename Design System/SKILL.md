## Brand Context
Vida Mágica is a Brazilian spiritual transformation and personal development brand by Suellen Seragi. The brand focuses on mental reprogramming, law of attraction, biblical prosperity, and spiritual awakening.

## Visual Identity
- **Color**: Warm golden amber (#C8922A), pearl white (#F5F0E8), blue morpho butterfly accent (#1E8FD5)
- **Typography**: Montserrat (display, bold, uppercase), Open Sans (body), Allura (script for names)
- **Aesthetic**: Ethereal golden bokeh backgrounds, light particles, blue butterflies, starburst symbolism
- **Mood**: Divine, premium, luxurious, warm, spiritual, transformational

## Key Design Elements
- Center-aligned compositions with radial symmetry
- Generous negative space and breathing room
- Soft shadows and golden glow effects
- Bokeh light particles creating magical atmosphere
- Blue morpho butterflies in lower right as transformation symbol
- 8-16px border radius for moderate softness

## Content Guidelines
- Language: Brazilian Portuguese
- Tone: Empowering, intimate, aspirational, spiritual but accessible
- Uses "você" (informal you) for warmth
- All caps for impactful headings
- Script font for names (Suellen Seragi)
- No emoji; uses photographic and illustrated elements instead

## Available Assets
- Logos (horizontal, vertical, favicon with starburst)
- Signature golden bokeh background with butterflies
- Blue morpho butterfly element
- 11 product cover images
- Complete CSS color and typography system

Import colors_and_type.css for full design system tokens. Reference assets from /assets directory. Follow README.md for comprehensive brand guidelines including CONTENT FUNDAMENTALS, VISUAL FOUNDATIONS, and ICONOGRAPHY sections.
